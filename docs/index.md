### 🩺 Step 1 — Start with Your Level
Choose the path that matches your experience:
- **Beginner:** Build a strong foundation — measurements, normal values, and image views.  
- **Intermediate:** Master Doppler, chamber quantification, and pathology correlations.  
- **Advanced:** Review stress echo, congenital heart disease, and board exam simulation.
