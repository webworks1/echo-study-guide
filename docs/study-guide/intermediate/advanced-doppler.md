# Advanced Doppler

## 1️⃣ Right-Heart & Pulmonary Pressures (ASE 2025)
| Parameter | Normal / Key Cutoff | ASE 2025 Notes |
|------------|--------------------|----------------|
| **TRV (m/s)** | < 2.8 | Use TRV + supportive echo signs for PH probability assessment |
| **TAPSE (cm)** | ≥ 1.7 | Frames RV systolic function severity |
| **FAC (%)** | ≥ 35 | < 35 = abnormal RV function |

> **ASE Reference:** JASE Mar 2025 — “Guidelines for the Echocardiographic Assessment of the Right Heart in Adults.”

## 2️⃣ Diastolic Function Highlights (ASE 2025)
| Measure | Normal | Elevated Filling Pressure |
|----------|---------|---------------------------|
| **Average E/e′** | < 10 | > 14 |
| **TRV (m/s)** | < 2.8 | ≥ 2.8 → supportive for ↑ LA pressure |

> **ASE Reference:** JASE Jul 2025 — “Recommendations for the Evaluation of Left Ventricular Diastolic Function by Echocardiography.”
